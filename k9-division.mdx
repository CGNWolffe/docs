# **👕 K9 Uniform Policy**

**Uniform Standard:** All K9 Constables must maintain a consistent tactical appearance while on patrol.

---

## **🎖️ K9 Constable Uniform**

**Required Uniform**

- Green ERT Uniform
- Standard Duty Belt
- Approved Tactical Footwear

**Mandatory:** All K9 Constables must be wearing the green ERT uniform while on patrol.

---

# **⚠️ K9 Rules**

- K9 vehicles are restricted to **certified handlers only**
- K9 deployment must be **realistic and justified**
- Handlers are responsible for **K9 control at all times**
- K9 use must follow **use-of-force guidelines**

---

**Policy:** Unauthorized K9 use or improper uniform may result in removal from the unit.
