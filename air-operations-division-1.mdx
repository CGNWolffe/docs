# Air Operations Division (AOD)

Specialized aviation unit focused on aerial patrol, tactical air support, search and rescue operations, and drone surveillance support.

---

## 🎖️ Air Operations Officer

### 🚓 Ground Support / Drone Unit Fleet

#### Air Operations Support Vehicles

- 2025 Ford Expedition (Drone Unit)

**Drone Unit Vehicles:** Used for mobile drone deployment, incident command support, aerial surveillance coordination, and tactical overwatch operations.

---

## 🚁 Aviation Fleet

### Rotary Wing Aircraft

- Airbus H135
- Sikorsky UH-60 Black Hawk

**Air Operations Aircraft:** Utilized for aerial patrol, tactical insertion, search and rescue missions, surveillance operations, medical evacuation assistance, and cross-border support operations.

---

## 📌 Air Operations Responsibilities

- Provide aerial support to Ontario Provincial Police operations
- Conduct aerial patrol and highway monitoring
- Assist ground units during pursuits and high-risk incidents
- Operate drone systems during tactical and investigative operations
- Conduct search and rescue missions
- Provide aerial overwatch during public events and large-scale incidents
- Assist Emergency Response Team and Criminal Investigations Branch operations
- Maintain aircraft readiness and operational professionalism
- Successfully complete flight and operational evaluations

---

## 📋 Qualifications

- Rank of **Senior Constable** or higher
- Minimum activity requirement of **3 days per week**
- Strong communication and report-writing skills
- Ability to operate effectively under pressure
- Must demonstrate professionalism and aviation maturity
- Completion of Air Operations training and evaluation process required

# 👕 Air Operations Division Uniform

## Uniform Standard

Air Operations personnel are required to maintain a professional aviation and tactical appearance while on active duty.

---

## 🎖️ Authorized Air Operations Uniform Combination

### Flight Operations Uniform

- Flight Suit or Air Operations Uniform
- Tactical Flight Vest
- Aviation Headset Equipment
- Approved Duty Belt
- Black Combat Boots

### Ground Drone Operations Uniform

- Patrol Jacket or Tactical Jacket
- Black Turtleneck
- Tactical or Standard Duty Belt
- Approved Footwear

**Appearance:** Air Operations personnel must maintain a clean, professional, and mission-ready appearance during all flight and support operations.

---

## 🚁 Optional Equipment

- Night Vision Equipment
- Aviation Helmets
- Drone Surveillance Systems
- Tactical Medical Kits
- Rescue Equipment
- Search Lights / FLIR Systems
- Aviation Communication Equipment

---

## ⚠️ Air Operations Rules

- Aircraft and drone units are restricted to authorized Air Operations personnel only
- Members must maintain realistic aviation and tactical RP at all times
- Aircraft may only be deployed for authorized operational purposes
- Pilots and drone operators are responsible for safe flight operations
- Unauthorized stunting or reckless flying is strictly prohibited
- Air Operations personnel are expected to maintain professionalism and activity standards

**Policy:** Failure to follow aviation procedures, operational standards, or professionalism requirements may result in immediate removal from Air Operations Division.

---

# 📢 IMPORTANT NOTICE

All applicants must demonstrate professionalism, maturity, communication ability, and strong operational awareness.

Failure to maintain Air Operations standards or department expectations may result in suspension or removal from the division.
