# Community Standards

Welcome to **Great Lakes Justice Roleplay (GLJRP)**.

GLJRP is a serious roleplay community focused on immersive law enforcement, emergency services, and civilian roleplay across the Great Lakes region of North America.

Our goal is to maintain:

- Professional roleplay
- Realistic emergency services interactions
- Balanced criminal and civilian scenarios
- A welcoming and respectful community

---

# Community Conduct

## Respect All Members

All members are expected to treat others with professionalism and respect at all times.

The following behavior is strictly prohibited:

- Harassment
- Hate speech
- Racism or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Toxic or disruptive behavior

GLJRP is intended to be a welcoming environment for serious roleplay.

---

## Advertising

Advertising other communities, products, or services without staff approval is prohibited.

This includes:

- Discord servers
- Other FiveM communities
- Personal businesses
- Promotional links

---

## Discord Terms of Service

All members must comply with Discord’s official Terms of Service.

Violations of Discord policies may result in immediate removal from the community.

---

# Discord Guidelines

Members are expected to use Discord responsibly.

## Server Rules

- Do not spam channels
- Keep discussions appropriate
- Use the correct channels
- Follow moderator instructions
- Avoid disruptive behavior

Staff reserve the right to remove content that negatively impacts the community.

---

# Core Roleplay Rules

## FailRP

FailRP refers to unrealistic behavior that breaks immersion.

### Examples

- Ignoring severe injuries
- Unrealistic driving without reason
- Charging into gunfire without fear
- Disregarding character safety

Players are expected to roleplay situations realistically.

---

## FearRP

Your character must value their life.

### Proper FearRP Examples

- Complying with armed threats
- Avoiding suicidal actions
- Acting realistically when outnumbered

Ignoring realistic threats may result in punishment.

---

## Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Killing or attacking another player without proper roleplay escalation is prohibited.

RDM violations may result in immediate administrative action.

---

## Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons intentionally is prohibited unless properly escalated within roleplay.

---

# Law Enforcement Roleplay

Members playing law enforcement must maintain professional standards.

## Officer Expectations

- Follow realistic police procedures
- Communicate professionally
- Follow use-of-force standards
- Respect escalation policies
- Maintain realistic conduct

Abuse of police authority is strictly prohibited.

---

# Pursuit Policy

Vehicle pursuits must remain realistic and controlled.

## Officers Must Consider

- Public safety
- Traffic conditions
- Environmental hazards
- Severity of the offense

## Prohibited Pursuit Behavior

- Unrealistic stunt driving
- Excessive vehicle ramming
- Exploiting game mechanics

Staff may terminate pursuits that become unrealistic.

---

# Criminal Roleplay

Criminal roleplay should create meaningful and balanced interactions.

## Criminal RP Rules

- Crimes must have realistic reasoning
- Excessive crime sprees are prohibited
- Cop baiting is prohibited
- Escalation standards must be followed which includes cool down on any shoot scenarios that involves LEO/EMS

Criminal roleplay should enhance overall server immersion.

---

# Major Crime Restrictions

To maintain balance and fairness:

- Chain robberies are prohibited
- Major robberies require planning
- Large-scale violent events require escalation approval

Staff may intervene if scenes become excessive or unrealistic.

---

# Civilian Roleplay

Civilian roleplay should contribute positively to server immersion.

## Civilian Guidelines

- Avoid trolling
- Avoid unrealistic behavior
- Do not intentionally disrupt scenes
- Follow escalation standards

Civilian interactions should improve the experience for all players.

---

# EMS & Fire Roleplay

Emergency services must maintain realistic operations.

## EMS Expectations

- Treat injuries appropriately
- Remain neutral during conflicts
- Prioritize medical roleplay

## Fire Department Expectations

- Respond realistically to emergencies
- Maintain professional conduct

## Restrictions

- No robbing EMS or Fire personnel
- No kidnapping EMS or Fire personnel
- Emergency personnel may not participate in combat scenarios

---

# Scene Escalation

Roleplay situations must escalate naturally.

## Example Escalation

```text
Traffic Stop
↓
Investigation
↓
Possible Arrest
↓
Use of Force (If Necessary)
```

Jumping directly to violence without proper escalation is prohibited.

---

# Metagaming & Power gaming

## Metagaming

Metagaming is the use of out-of-character information within roleplay.

### Examples

- Using Discord voice channels for in-character information
- Watching streams to locate players
- Acting on information your character does not know

---

## Power gaming

Power gaming occurs when players force outcomes unfairly.

### Examples

- Controlling another player’s actions
- Forcing roleplay outcomes
- Ignoring roleplay responses

Roleplay must remain interactive and fair for all participants.

---

# Punishment System

GLJRP follows a progressive discipline system.

| Offense Level | Punishment |
| :-- | :-- |
| First Offense | Warning |
| Second Offense | Temporary Suspension |
| Third Offense | Temporary Ban |
| Severe Violations | Permanent Ban |

## Severe Violations Include

- Exploiting
- Cheating
- Harassment
- Combat logging

---

# Staff Authority

Server staff are responsible for maintaining order and enforcing community standards.

## If You Disagree With Staff Action

- Remain respectful
- Submit an appeal through support channels
- Do not argue publicly

Staff decisions are final unless overturned through the appeal process.

---

Great Lakes Justice Roleplay is committed to maintaining a professional and immersive roleplay environment.
