---
title: "Emergency Response Team (ERT)"
---

Specialized tactical unit focused on high-risk incidents, rapid response operations, and advanced enforcement support.

---

## 🎖️ Emergency Response Team Operator

### 🚓 Tactical Response Vehicles

#### Marked / Tactical Units

- 2025 Ford F-250
- 2025 Ford F-350
- 2025 Ford Explorer
- 2025 Dodge Charger
- 2023 GMC Denali

#### Armored Response Vehicle

- Lenco Bearcat

**Tactical Units:** Used for high-risk responses, warrant executions, tactical deployments, and rapid support operations.

**Bearcat Usage:** Restricted to authorized tactical situations including armed suspects, barricaded subjects, active threats, and high-risk operations.

---

## 📌 ERT Responsibilities

- Provide tactical support during high-risk incidents
- Respond to barricaded suspects, riots, and warrant executions
- Conduct VIP and delegate escorts, including cross-border operations
- Assist frontline patrol units during critical incidents
- Maintain standard patrol duties while on active shift
- Successfully complete a mandatory 2-day tryout selection process
- Maintain professionalism and tactical readiness at all times

---

## 📋 Qualifications

- Rank of **Senior Constable** or higher
- Minimum activity requirement of **3 days per week**
- Strong report-writing and communication skills
- Ability to operate effectively under pressure
- Must pass ERT tryout and evaluation process

---

# 👕 Emergency Response Team Uniform

## Uniform Standard

ERT Operators are required to maintain a professional tactical appearance while deployed on patrol or tactical operations.

---

## 🎖️ Authorized ERT Uniform Combination

- Green ERT Uniform
- Tactical Vest / Plate Carrier
- Black Combat Boots
- Approved Tactical Belt
- Tactical Gloves (Optional)
- Authorized Helmet or Ballistic Protection (when required)

**Appearance:** The ERT uniform is intended to provide a professional, tactical, and identifiable presence during high-risk operations.

---

## 🚓 Optional Tactical Equipment

- Ballistic Helmet
- Gas Mask
- Shield Equipment
- Tactical Rifle Platform
- Less-Lethal Equipment
- Breaching Equipment
- Night Vision / Tactical Lighting

---

## ⚠️ ERT Rules

- Tactical vehicles are restricted to authorized ERT members only
- Bearcat deployment requires supervisor or command approval unless immediate threat exists
- Members must maintain realistic tactical RP at all times
- No unauthorized tactical equipment or uniforms
- Tactical callouts take priority over standard patrol duties
- Operators are expected to maintain discipline, professionalism, and activity standards

**Policy:** Failure to maintain professionalism, activity, or operational standards may result in removal from the Emergency Response Team.

---

# 📢 IMPORTANT NOTICE

All applicants must demonstrate professionalism, activity, tactical maturity, and strong report-writing ability.

Failure to meet unit expectations may result in suspension or removal from ERT operations.
