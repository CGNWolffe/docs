## About GLJRP\
\
Great Lakes Justice Roleplay (GLJRP) is a serious roleplay community focused on realistic law enforcement, emergency services, and civilian interactions throughout the Great Lakes region of North America.\
\
Our community emphasizes:\
\
- Professional roleplay\
- Realistic policing standards\
- Balanced criminal activity\
- Respectful community interaction\
