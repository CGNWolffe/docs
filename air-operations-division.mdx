---
title: "Criminal Investigations Branch (CIB)"
---

Specialized investigative unit focused on criminal investigations, intelligence gathering, covert operations, and case management.

---

## 🎖️ Criminal Investigations Branch Detective

### 🚓 Investigative Vehicles

#### Unmarked / Undercover Units

- 2025 Ford Explorer
- 2018 Ford Taurus
- 2025 Dodge Durango
- 2025 Dodge Charger
- 2012 Ford Crown Victoria

#### Specialized Investigation Units

- Unmarked SUVs
- Low-Profile Sedans
- Surveillance Vehicles

**Investigative Units:** Used for covert surveillance, criminal investigations, intelligence operations, suspect monitoring, and warrant follow-ups.

**Undercover Vehicles:** Designed for low-visibility operations and investigative assignments.

---

## 📌 CIB Responsibilities

- Conduct criminal investigations and follow-up cases
- Gather intelligence and evidence related to criminal activity
- Execute search warrants and investigative operations
- Conduct covert surveillance and undercover operations
- Prepare detailed reports and case files
- Coordinate with patrol and tactical divisions during investigations
- Assist with major crimes, gang investigations, narcotics, and organized crime operations
- Maintain professionalism and confidentiality during all investigations

---

## 📋 Qualifications

- Rank of **Senior Constable** or higher
- Minimum activity requirement of **3 days per week**
- Strong report-writing and investigative skills
- Professional conduct and communication ability
- Ability to maintain confidentiality and operational security
- Must pass CIB evaluation and interview process

---

# 👕 Criminal Investigations Branch Uniform

## Uniform Standard

CIB Detectives are required to maintain a professional appearance while conducting investigative and covert operations.

---

## 🎖️ Authorized CIB Uniform Combination

### Standard Investigative Attire

- Plain Clothes / Professional Attire
- Concealed Tactical Vest (when authorized)
- Badge & Credentials
- Approved Duty Belt or Concealed Carry Setup
- Professional Footwear

### Tactical Investigation Attire (When Required)

- Black Tactical Uniform
- Tactical Vest / Plate Carrier
- Approved Tactical Equipment

**Appearance:** CIB members are expected to maintain a clean, professional, and investigative-focused appearance during all operations.

---

## 🚓 Optional Investigative Equipment

- Surveillance Equipment
- Covert Recording Equipment
- Camera Systems
- Evidence Collection Kits
- Unmarked Emergency Lighting
- Undercover Communication Devices

---

## ⚠️ CIB Rules

- Investigative vehicles are restricted to authorized CIB members only
- Undercover operations must remain confidential
- Members must maintain realistic investigative RP at all times
- No unauthorized tactical gear or police markings on undercover vehicles
- Professionalism and confidentiality are mandatory
- Detectives are expected to maintain detailed and accurate reports

**Policy:** Unauthorized disclosure of investigations, misuse of investigative equipment, or failure to maintain professionalism may result in immediate removal from the Criminal Investigations Branch.

---

# 📢 IMPORTANT NOTICE

All applicants must demonstrate professionalism, maturity, investigative ability, and strong report-writing skills.

Failure to maintain investigative standards or department expectations may result in suspension or removal from CIB operations.
